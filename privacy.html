Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

Overview

Zangr ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how Zangr handles your information.

Data Collection

Zangr does not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on external servers. We have no backend servers and no user accounts.

All data you create within Zangr — including collections, items, photos, and tags — is stored exclusively on your device.

Data Storage

Your data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. We do not have access to your data at any time.

Backup files exported from Zangr are stored wherever you choose to save them (e.g., your Files app, iCloud Drive, or email). You are solely responsible for the security of your backup files.

Photos and Media

When you add photos to Zangr, the app copies those photos to its local storage on your device. These photos are never transmitted to any server or third party.

Private Collections

Private collections are protected by a password you set. The password is stored locally on your device using iOS's UserDefaults system. We do not have access to your passwords or the content of your private collections.

In-App Purchases

Zangr uses Apple's StoreKit framework to process in-app purchases. All payment processing is handled by Apple. We do not collect or store any payment information.

Analytics and Tracking

Zangr does not use any analytics tools, tracking software, or advertising SDKs. We do not track your usage of the app.

Children's Privacy

Zangr is rated 17+ and is not intended for use by children under 17. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the date at the top of this page.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the App Store.
